Where is my supervisor?

This is your second week as a trainee in a posh downtown restaurant in Manchester. Everything is going well, you like the owner who is kind to you, as well as your new colleagues. For the duration of your traineeship, you are attached to a supervisor - a nice young woman named Rose. She is one of the four waitresses in the restaurant. Since you are here to help her, Rose manages 7 tables with guests. Each of the other waitresses manages 5 tables.

However, some problems are waiting for you and it seems that it will not be an easy day.

  • OK, tell me the problem

  • When you come to work Monday noon, everything looks as usual. However, Rose does not turn up for her shift. She does not answer her work mobile. You do not have her private mobile number. Things get even worse, as there are more clients than usual and you can hardly manage their orders. The other waitresses probably cannot help, as they seem to be barely managing servicing their own tables.

  • I will try to manage the situation myself. Rose will probably come soon and she will be thankful that I have covered for her. Clients may wait a little longer but I will manage somehow.
